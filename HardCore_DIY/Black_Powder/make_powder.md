
# How to make black powder.
# 如何制作黑火药.  

#### 1.第一件事情当然是准备原材料  

原材料有三种:  

- 碳 -- C
![碳](https://cbu01.alicdn.com/img/ibank/2017/430/564/4086465034_338488334.jpg)
- 硫 -- S
![硫](https://ts1.cn.mm.bing.net/th/id/R-C.dc6e7f1f09d842e16d22528d83cd1c3a?rik=xEs9pmalWMiDuw&riu=http%3a%2f%2fwww.hnqxhg.com%2fdata%2fupload%2fimage%2f20190618%2f1560824261768684.jpg&ehk=VwucUeuiIyNa2NrSebBdMX%2fGS6dft5RIvmOIxrU7pAw%3d&risl=&pid=ImgRaw&r=0)
- 硝酸钾 -- KNO~3~
![硝酸钾](https://p1.ssl.qhimg.com/t016ec3ada6c3611eee.png)

#### 2.单独磨粉 

把三种原料分别磨成粉末,越细越好.  

#### 3.称量原料  

按质量之比,每份火药中:
  
- 75%硝
- 15%碳
- 10%硫

![称量](https://www.wikihow.com/images_en/thumb/d/d8/Make-Gunpowder-Step-5-preview.jpg/550px-nowatermark-Make-Gunpowder-Step-5-preview.jpg.webp)

#### 4.混合磨粉

将各原料混合后,继续磨粉,保证原料混合均匀

![混合磨粉](https://www.wikihow.com/images_en/thumb/b/b2/Make-Gunpowder-Step-6-preview.jpg/550px-nowatermark-Make-Gunpowder-Step-6-preview.jpg.webp)

- 在黑火药一经混合,在其周围一定要当心.在火药附近不要抽烟或者点火,使其远离热源.

## 这样你就拥有黑火药啦!!!
